About Similarity Engine

Similarity Engine is programe that can that can find/extract similar text in a corpus and flag it for verification.

For example:

STEAL AND STREETS ASSOCIATES and STEAL and S Associates L.L.C will be flagged as possible similar items

CAPTRIP and CAPSTRIP 12 mm will be flagged as possible similar items

Copper screw, galvanized, 3/4 in. and : .75 screw; copper; gal will be flagged as possible similar items
